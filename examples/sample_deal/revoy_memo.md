## One-liner

Revoy is an electric add-on dolly that converts existing diesel semi-trucks into hybrid-electrics in under 5 minutes, with no modifications to the truck. The economics work because the same fleet operator keeps their existing tractor and reduces fuel cost by ~50% on long-haul routes.

## Why now

- Diesel #2 averaging $4.10/gal in 2026, with EPA Phase 3 GHG rules tightening fleet emissions starting 2027
- Battery-electric Class 8 tractors (Tesla Semi, Freightliner eCascadia) still cost $400K+ and have <300mi range with payload
- "Messy middle" thesis: hybrid retrofits are 18-36 month bridge while charging infrastructure builds out
