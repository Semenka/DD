# Acme Robotics raises $20M Series A from Andreessen Horowitz

Acme Robotics, the warehouse robotics startup, has raised $20M Series A led by Andreessen Horowitz (a16z), with participation from existing seed investors Lux Capital and Khosla Ventures, the company announced today.

Founded in 2022 by [Jane Smith](https://linkedin.com/in/janesmith), a former Boston Dynamics engineer, Acme builds an autonomous bin-picking robot that reduces warehouse pick rates by 4x compared to human pickers.
